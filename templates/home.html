Why
MathArt challenges you to use both sides of your brain to make an original artwork that connects Mathematics in a visual and creative way to the theme of the competition. This year's theme is "My Universe"
This project aims to promote STEAM education in South African Schools because Creative problem-solving is an essential job skill for the 4IR.
When
Who
The Competition opens on 3 March 2020 and closes on 29 April 2020 at 5pm. Early bird submissions will be entered into a lucky draw.
All learners from Grade 7 to 12 that live in South Africa can enter.
How Much
Entry is FREE
