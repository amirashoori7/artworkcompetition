HOW TO ENTER


Step 1 Consider the theme "My Universe" and what it means to you.
Step 2 Look around you and find Maths in "My Universe".
Step 3 Create an artwork showing the Maths you have found.
Step 4 Take a photo of your artwork and keep the original artwork safe for later.

Step 5 Think about the following questions:

1) What Mathematics did you use in your artwork? Explain your answer .
2) How did you use creativity to connect Mathematics with "My Universe" to make your artwork?
3) Do you think creativity could be used to teach Mathematics? Explain your answer.

Step 6 Read and make sure you understand and agree to comply with the Competition Rules
Step 7 Double check you have all the information you need to enter with you.
Step 8 Complete and submit your entry form and photo of your artwork online HERE!
