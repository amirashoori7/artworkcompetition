Frequently Asked Questions


    About MathArt
   Is this Maths competition or an Art competition?
   Neither! It is a combination of both. We are looking for the original way you combine Maths and Art. Both the image of the artwork and your answers to the questions will be considered during judging.
   What do you mean "My Universe"?
   We would like you to show us what you consider YOUR Universe. It could be your private universe, your internal universe, your immediate universe, the cosmic universe or any other interpretation. Be creative and original and make sure you clearly explain in your answers.
   Where can I find out more about MathArt?
    Follow us on Facebook, Youtube and Instagram for more links and inspiration.
     Who Can Enter?
   Who can enter?
   All grade 7's to 12' s who go to school within the borders of South Africa.
   What do I need to enter?
    You need a valid email address and the contact details of your Parent/Guardian to Register.
   Can we enter as a group?
    No, not at this time.
   I don't have an email address. Can I still enter?
   No, you need to sign up to Gmail or similar for an email address, or ask a friend or relative to help you use their email address.
   I take Mathematical Literacy at school, can I still enter?
   Yes, definitely, Maths is everywhere for everyone.
   I am in Grade 6 or below, can I still enter?
   You may enter, but your work will be judged in the Grade 7 category.
   I am home-schooled how do I enter?
   
   I live in a SADC country can I still enter?
   Only if you go to school in Swaziland or Lesotho.
     What Can I Enter
   Photography is art, can I submit a photograph as my artwork?
   No. You must take a true-to-life photo of a physical artwork you have hand-made yourself.
   Must I make a physical artwork or is this a digital competition?
   No, computer aided designs or images will be accepted.
   Can I enter more than one artwork?
    No, not this time.
     The Entry Process
   Can I enter from my phone?
   Yes.
   I have entered before, why is this year different?
    Our Competition is growing each year. Online submissions are the best way for us to reach more learners and schools across the whole country.
   Who must send their art to Port Elizabeth?
    We will inform the entrants whose artwork has been selected for the next round of judging in Port Elizabeth.
   How do you send art to Port Elizabeth?
    We will explain all those details if your artwork is selected.
   What if I cant afford to send my art to Port Elizabeth?
    You can apply for financial assistance to get it to us. We will tell you how if your artwork is selected.
   What can I win?
    A trip to Port Elizabeth to the Prizegiving and vouchers for Maths and Art equipment.
     Troubleshooting
   My data ran out and I have lost my entry, what must I do?
   Any time during the time when registration is open, you can come back, login to your account and submit the entry form.
   My battery died and I didn't finish submitting my entry, what must I do?
   Login to your account and submit the entry form again once you had at least 50MB of data.
